Abigail Pusey, President

My love for construction and renovation came to me when I was teenager. I grew up in Pennsburg, Pennsylvania – a small town northwest of Philadelphia – in a fixer-upper my parents bought in 1999. Bored and itching for something exciting to pass the time, I find myself removing wallpaper per my father’s (almost sarcastic) suggestion. And guess what? I was hooked! I continued to renovate our home until I moved out. I spent my high school classes daydreaming of the weekends and summer in which I’d put in more work. Now, I’m grateful to have turned my passion into a full-time career. In my free time, I love to read, spend time with my girls, and hike outdoors every chance I can get.

The Company

Abby Contracting, LLC was created amidst this volatile, unprecedented post-COVID-19 world. In order to adapt, Abigail sought opportunity with the help of a great friend and inspiration, Denny, and developed the blueprints of what is now a successful contracting business. We are here to proudly serve all your home construction needs.
