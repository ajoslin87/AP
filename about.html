I grew up in Pennsburg, Pennsylvania – a small town northwest of Philadelphia – in a fixer-upper my parents bought in 1999. My love for construction and renovation came to me when I was 14. I was bored and itched for something exciting to pass the time. What does my dad tell me I can do? Remove wallpaper. And guess what? I was hooked. I continued to renovate our home until I moved out in 2016. I spent class time in high school daydreaming of the weekends and summer when I could put in more work.
